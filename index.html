How to create a datGo link

Make it easy for your social media followers to access your dat sites.

Just add the following url with your hash to your favorite post.


          https://datgo.hashbase.io?dat=[your hash]

This will send your followers to this page encouraging them to download and install beaker browser then click on your link to access your dat site.

Once BeakerBrowser is installed your users will be able to go straight to the dat site in Beaker.
